Owner

I am the owner and main operator of 802 Sitters. Ever since I was a kid I’ve always wanted to be able to take care of other peoples animals, due to the fact we could not have animals in our house growing up. So I made it my mission to help neighbors and friends pets while they were away on vacation and trips! This rewarding hobby as a young adult as now bloomed into an amazing business where I can help your pets feel at ease while you are away. I have a cat with my wife, named Mia, who has taught me a great deal about pet ownership over the last 7 years.

Since the early 2000’s, we’ve had the pleasure and privilege to care for animals and their homes.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide top-quality home and pet sitting services that give pet owners peace of mind while they are away from home. We believe that pets are an important part of our lives, and we strive to ensure they receive the love and attention they need while their owners are away. Our goal is to build strong relationships with our clients and their furry family members by providing reliable, trustworthy, and personalized care. We are committed to creating a safe, secure, and comfortable environment for both pets and their owners, and we aim to exceed our clients' expectations with every visit.
